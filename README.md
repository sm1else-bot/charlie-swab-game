## CHARLIE SWAB
### Feature Overview

**Charlie Swab** is a narrative-driven stock market simulation game spanning nearly two decades of modern financial history. Players navigate volatile markets, personal pressure, and systemic risk while attempting to build wealth from the 2008 financial crisis onward.

---

## Core Simulation

- 30 tradable stocks, each with distinct volatility profiles, sector classifications, and narrative descriptions  
- 19 industry sectors, including energy, technology, finance, and crypto, each with custom display names  
- 18+ year historical timeline covering 2008–2026  
- Weekly market simulation with 52 trading weeks per year  

---

## Visual Progression

- 34 unique background environments distributed across four wealth tiers:
  - Tier 0 (Below $50,000): 11 environments representing a struggling apartment
  - Tier 1 ($50,000–$100,000): 8 environments reflecting modest improvement
  - Tier 2 ($100,000–$500,000): 6 environments depicting financial stability
  - Tier 3 ($500,000+): 9 luxury environments
- Dynamic background rotation that updates weekly based on player progress  

---

## Audio System

- Dynamic soundtrack with three distinct tracks tied to net worth tiers
- Automatic music transitions as wealth milestones are crossed
- Player-controlled mute and unmute functionality  

---

## Events and News

### Scripted Events
- 23 hand-crafted “lucky” and disruptive events distributed across early game years
- Events include corporate scandals, technological breakthroughs, market shocks, and speculative booms

### Historical Market Events
- 13 historically grounded events spanning 2008–2026, including:
  - The 2008 financial crisis
  - Market flash crashes
  - Credit rating downgrades
  - The COVID-19 pandemic
  - Inflation-driven downturns
  - Major tariff actions
  - Dow Jones milestone movements

### AI-Generated News
- 0–5 dynamically generated news headlines per in-game week
- Headlines adapt to the current year, portfolio composition, and market context

---

## Financial Systems

### Trading Mechanics
- Dedicated buy and sell modals with full quantity control
- Real-time portfolio tracking with average cost basis calculation
- Per-stock profit and loss analytics with percentage gain display
- Persistent transaction log showing the five most recent trades  

### Wealth Milestones
- $50,000: Insider trading system unlocked
- $100,000: Six-figure milestone
- $250,000: Quarter-million status
- $500,000: Half-million status
- $1,000,000: Millionaire tier reached  

---

## Advanced Gameplay Systems

### Insider Trading
- Unlocks at $50,000 net worth
- Costs $500 per tip
- 70% probability of accuracy
- Tips provide one-week advance signals
- AI-generated cryptic messaging

### Private Equity and Startups
- AI-generated startup pitches with historically appropriate context
- Each pitch includes:
  - Founder identities
  - Company concepts
  - Investment ranges ($1,000–$15,000)
  - Equity stakes (5–49%)
  - Success probabilities (1–99%)
- Quarterly dividend payouts every 13 weeks
- Dynamic valuation growth or decline based on outcomes
- Hidden Supercell easter egg event in 2009, Week 36

### Board Membership
- Triggered by owning 10,000+ shares of a single stock
- AI-generated boardroom decisions and strategic dilemmas
- Player voting directly influences stock price movement
- Unique executive characters per company  

---

## Personal Pressure Systems

### Rent Obligations
- Rent due every 12 weeks
- $900/month during 2008–2010
- $1,000/month after 2011 to reflect inflation
- First payment due Week 10 (March 2008)
- Retro pager-style notifications

### Family Requests
- Annual random family money requests beginning Week 17
- AI-generated characters and scenarios
- Request amounts range from $100 to $800
- Player choice to assist or block requests
- Persistent character identities across runs  

---

## Game Structure

### Views
- Apartment view with interactive computer terminal
- Trading terminal featuring full market interface  

### Time Progression
- Advance one week with full simulation detail
- Skip one month (four weeks) with summarized recap  

### Modal Interfaces
- Intro and narrative setup
- Buy and sell trading interfaces
- Weekly news delivery
- Monthly recap summaries
- Messaging and notification modals
- Rent, family, board, and startup interactions
- Milestone celebrations and bankruptcy screen

## Win and Loss Conditions

- Game over occurs when net worth falls below zero
- Soft victory condition upon reaching $1,000,000 net worth
- Full restart available after bankruptcy  

---

## Developer Utilities

- Cheat codes:
  - `timetravel` opens a time navigation modal
  - `amirichyet` grants an immediate $25,000 cash bonus
- Time travel modal allows jumping to any year or week  

---

## Market Modeling

- Per-stock volatility ranging from 0.02 to 0.60
- Sector-based movement correlations
- Era-specific market bias:
  - 2008–2009: Recession phase
  - 2010–2012: Recovery phase
  - 2013–2019: Bull market
  - 2020: Pandemic shock
  - 2021: Speculative boom
  - 2022: Inflation-driven contraction
  - 2023 onward: Sustained growth phase
